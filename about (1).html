About Saraswathy Bakery

A trusted neighborhood store serving the local community since 2008

Our Story

Saraswathy Bakery was established in 2008 by Kishore Kumar with a vision to create a one-stop shop that would serve the everyday needs of the local community. What started as a small bakery has evolved into a beloved neighborhood store offering a wide range of products including groceries, bakery items, snacks, and stationery.

Named after the goddess of knowledge, Saraswathy, our bakery embodies the values of wisdom and service. We've grown with the community, adapting to their needs while maintaining our commitment to quality and affordability.

Today, Saraswathy Bakery stands as a trusted establishment in Thirumullavaram, Kollam, where customers from all walks of life find products that meet their daily needs and occasional indulgences.

To provide everyday essentials and delicious snacks to the local community at affordable prices while maintaining the highest standards of quality and service.

Customer-Centric Approach

We prioritize customer satisfaction in everything we do. From carefully selecting our product range to training our staff, we ensure that every customer interaction leaves a positive impression.

Supporting Local

We take pride in sourcing some of our products from local suppliers, thereby supporting the local economy and ensuring freshness and quality.
